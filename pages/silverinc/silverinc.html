Introduction

The aim of this project is to combine traditional FMD 3D printing with incjet printing. Using a conductive inc we should then be able to print small electronics. Imagine breaking a remote for your TV, but instead of spending both money and time getting a replacement, you could just download a model off the internet, and print it yourself. Or people who are good with 3D modelling software would be able to create their own custom remote control.

These are still early days, so there is not much to write here yet.
